CHARACTERS

MUADDI

Introducing Muaddi, the perfect weapon of the Red Beacon and the tragic heroine of Anthill. She used to be the nemesis of the corporations until they transformed her into their most potent weapon through the power of biohacking technology. Her body serves as the core component of Stasis, a cutting-edge quantum teleporter that allows Farcana's stars to avoid risking their lives in the arena.

Q

Introducing Q, the last person to leave Earth. Born in the shadows beneath the ground, Q fled Earth as an illegal passenger on the last ship. Unable to see the sky above the earth, he strived to conquer the skies of Mars. Q vowed not to abandon those in distress, as the earthlings had abandoned him. His wings, ambitions, and maximalism led him to where any dream is possible - to Farcana.

CERBERUS

Meet Cerberus, the formidable force behind Mars' security and prosperity. He granted the planet its freedom and a place in the sun. But at a cost. His brutal methods of clearing Mars of unlawful settlers and liberating corporate lands earned his family a place on the planet, but he wasn't ready to give up his own freedom. In a bold move, he eliminated his squad of thugs, the last criminals on Mars, and sought a fresh start in Farcana—a place of redemption.

DIANA

Meet Diana — the voice of humanity! Her participation in the game sends shivers down the spine of all earthlings in orbit. In the arena, the politician defends the interests of the entire human race. Diana is ready to prove that her words carry weight. She will participate in the games herself, and if she wins, she'll award the prize to a lucky person in orbit. Her death could lead to war, so her victory is so important. Follow the heroic protector whose participation can change the world!

TEACHER

Meet Teacher — a bright, talented, and kind guy who grew up in a small village orphanage in the Philippines. From childhood, he had a passion for drawing, and his talent was noticed by a teacher who supported and inspired him to become a teacher himself. When the shelter where he grew up was closed, our hero and the other wards organized a protest. They created beautiful posters and kites and took to the streets. This "Colorful Protest" unexpectedly turned into the most massive and bloody riot in history. Teacher ended up in a juvenile prison, where he began teaching inmates drawing, literature, and history. After his release, he was unable to get a teaching job due to his criminal record. However, he continued to help street children by organizing free lessons in public parks.

SHERWOOD

Amelia Sherwood used to be the youngest captain in the Air Force. Sherwood did not fail a single time during her service in the Air Force. She had to make hard decisions all the time, and they didn't always have the best outcomes. She was indeed a good soldier, but was she a good person? Doubt began creeping in.
